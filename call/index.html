Call for abstracts

We are very happy to invite you to the 2021 conference, which will be organized by our group in Paris. This call goes out relatively late (and we apologize for this) because we are still very much in the decision process of whether it will be possible to have at least some part of the conference in Paris.

We decided to organize a “hybrid format” in which the entire conference will be accessible online. This includes the possibility to present and participate in discussions remotely. We will, however, keep up the option of some in-person participation if circumstances permit it.

We will follow the format of this year’s CUNY conference with a main session with 30 min slots (20 min talk, 10 min discussion) and thematically organized parallel sessions with short (5 min) talks and a grouped discussion. These parallel sessions will replace poster sessions.

Abstracts can be submitted here from April 10 through May 17.
